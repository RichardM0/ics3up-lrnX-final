MIT: Introduction to Computer Science with Python

This course teaches you about the many Python datatypes such as strings, ints, floats, tuples, lists, and more. It shows you Object Orientated Programming, complexity, loops, functions, recursions, and everything in basic-intermediate python. Taught by Eric Grimson, it includes exercise packages, questions, lectures, and examples on the course material.
  • 9 weeks (14 hours per week) Instructed by Eric Grimson
