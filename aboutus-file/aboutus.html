What is lrnX?

lrnX is a website that provides courses from a multitude of businesses, schools, and programs. These courses all require a payment upfront to access the content as it is their courses and not ours. We are not sponsored in anyway, but have gained the rights to distribute the courses at a paid price, which a percentage of the profits go back to the schools and charities.
